Our Mission

At Access Care, our mission is to provide exceptional housekeeping and caregiving services with a commitment to transparency, integrity, and personalized care. We strive to enhance the well-being and quality of life for our clients, offering reliable and compassionate support tailored to each individual's needs.
Our Vision
Our vision is to be the leading provider of transparent and trustworthy housekeeping and caregiving services, fostering a community where every individual feels valued, supported, and empowered to live their best life.
Our History
Access Care was founded by a dedicated group of six individuals—Eloissa, Jay-Ann, Tara, Signet, Keziah, and Angel—who shared a common passion for helping others and a vision of creating a service rooted in trust and excellence. Our journey began with the simple belief that everyone deserves quality care and a clean, comfortable home. Together, we combined our diverse skills and experiences to establish Access Care, a system designed to provide top-notch housekeeping and caregiving services with complete transparency. Since our inception, we have grown into a trusted name in the community, always staying true to our founding principles of compassion, reliability, and open communication.
